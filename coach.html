Program Frequently Asked Questions

Who needs to be certified? expand_more

Every head coach and assistant coach. Certification means having passed the appropriate age-division class, Safe Haven, and Concussion Awareness training. Multiple assistants must all be certified to work with players during practice.

I don't know anything about soccer. Can I still coach? expand_more

Yes! AYSO provides comprehensive training and materials. Being a coach involves management and mentorship skills you probably already have. Start as an assistant coach to learn alongside an experienced peer!

What is the weekly time commitment? expand_more

Typically, coaches run one training session and one game per week. Your Local Regional Commissioner can provide specifics on local routines.
